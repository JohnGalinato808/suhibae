ABOUT SUSHI BAY


Conveyor belt style sushi restaurant serving a variety of sushi, sashimi, udon noodles, beef, chicken and other cooked items. Offering quality, good portions at an affordable price. Please stop by...you won't be disappointed!



HISTORY: Established in 2013. Lance Yamamoto, previously employed at Genki Sushi for 11 years and Kuru Kuru Sushi for 8 years, has taken over as owner of Sushi Bay from February of this year. His reputation and knowledge of the Kaiten "conveyor belt" type sushi restaurant will satisfy your sushi cravings. Compared to other conveyor belt sushi restaurants on the island, Sushi Bay provides quality materials with good portions at an affodable price. Please give it a try the next time you are on the west side!


OWNER: Lance Y. Born and raised in Hawaii, Lance graduated from the University of Hawaii at Manoa with a business degree and has worked various restaurants including 19 years combined with Genki Sushi and Kuru Kuru Sushi. Trained in Japan and has now ventured on his own and bought the Sushi Bay Restaurant in Kapolei as of February 2013. He understands the local market and will do his best to provide affordable, quality sushi to everyone coming to his restaurant.
